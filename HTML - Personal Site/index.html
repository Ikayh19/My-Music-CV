Ikayh

Artiste and song writer at Hiltz Entertainment Spotify

Ikechukwu Ejiofor known professionally as Ikayh is a Nigerian singer and songwriter. He started music when he was 14 years old and professionally started recording music in 2015, he released his debut EP titled “The Life Of Ikayh” in 2020 which received positive reviews and his lead Single “Toxic” being top 10 songs of 2020 from rising acts and his second single “Alcohol” being one of his biggest songs off the project with over 99,000 streams on Spotify, Ikayh’s storytelling is nothing short of amazing and he sites Burna Boy as one of his major influences in the music industry.
